Understanding the Visualization

  • You can click on the Street Name and the map will auto zoom to focus on the location. When there is only 1 (one) occurrence, the map zoom out but you can easily pan/zoom the map manually.
  • The bubbles represent the reported location â€“ which might not be 100% accurate. A cluster of points usually represent only one actual pothole.
  • The color saturation is based on the Reliability Score: the darker, the more reliable is the information.

  • When hovering the bubbles on the map, additional information will be provided, such as:
    • Street name
    • Date and Time of creation
    • How many days have passed since the Pothole was first reported
