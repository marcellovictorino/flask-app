Understanding the Visualization

  • The Dataset is an integration between Waze and Cityworks.
  • Waze: Potholes are reported by users â€“ which might not be 100% accurate. A cluster of points usually represents only one actual pothole. Recommended: Reliability > 6.
  • Cityworks: Composed of Service Requests and Work Orders.

  • The Dashbord has 3 sections: 1) Cityworks only; 2) Waze only; and 3) Integration of both data sources.
  • The map shows Potholes by Source. But it can be drilled down and display Pothole by Status (Open, Closed, In Progress etc.)

  • When hovering the bubbles on the map, additional information will be provided, such as:
    • Source
    • Reliability Score
    • Date and Time of creation
    • Respective SR/WO id#
    • How many days have passed since the Pothole was first reported

Understanding the Slicers

  • Date is relative to current time. It can be changed to Years, Months, or Weeks.
  • Waze Reliability Score interval can be modified, simply enter the value or adjust the slider.
  • In order to reduce the probability of Waze duplicates, it is recommended to use Reliability > 6
