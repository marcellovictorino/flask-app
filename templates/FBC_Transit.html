Understanding the Visualization

  • There are two (2) tabs in this Dashboard: the first one for the Commuter Service and the second one for the Demand Response service.
  • The data is updated around the 10th of every month, based on the operational data of the previous second month.

  • When hovering the bubbles on the map, additional information will be provided, such as:
    • Street name
    • Date and Time of creation
    • How many days have passed since the Pothole was first reported
